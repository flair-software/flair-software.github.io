Path to launching good product

1

Share your idea with us, we can keep the secret

We need to know what are you planning, so that we can do our job with understanding and passion we got used to. Fill contact form to reach us or just write an email.

2

Building the product backlog

Your idea is a composition of stories that you wish users will realize with your product. We need to define them to understand goals, interactions, dependencies and priorities. We have a set of tools that let us to do it with you. Distance in kilometers and time zones are not a problems for us.

Have you heard about Pareto Principle? So you know that aiming at the right 20% of scope can be a difference between success and failure. This is also a difference between tailored and expensive product.

3

Tell us how much you want to spend or maybe nothing

You tell us how much you want to spend and we can tell you how much we can deliver for that. If you wish, we can of course do it other way - we can tell you how much it will cost. You may wish to sign Time and Materials contract as well.

4

Our spirit is agilility

We find it natural to follow agile manifesto, so you should expect transparency, solid software craftmanship and iterations. After each of them you receive potentially shippable product. You can validate it and decide where to go next. Be lean with us.

5

We deliver MVP

You receive MVP that is ready to hit the market or prototype to show to focus groups. We can host product for you. We can support it and maintain if you wish.

5

Did you like it?

We will appreciate if you spend some time with us at the end for retrospective, to look how our cooperation went.
